Workers

Homerton’s biggest and best May Ball yet takes place on 16th June 2017. In order to make it as spectacular and brilliant as we know it will be, we need your help! If you are interested in working at Homerton May Ball 2017, please check out the roles we have available—applications open later in January, so watch this space!
