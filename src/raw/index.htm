Roll up, roll up to the House of Fun! Homerton May Ball 2017 will kick off May Week in style, with a full night of dazzling entertainment. Magnificent music, delicious food and drink, and the unrivalled spectacle of the fair will transform our beautiful college grounds—prepare for a night like no other. Come one, come all!
  • Friday 16th June
  • 9:00pm–5:00am
  • Black Tie

Tickets

Ticket applications will open at 8pm on Saturday 28th January to current students of Homerton College, and at 8pm on Saturday 4th February to all University students and Homerton alumni. Successful applicants will have one week to make payment before their reserved ticket(s) are released to the waiting list.

Each ticket applicant can buy up to three guest tickets in addition to their own. These tickets may be for current students at any college, alumni, or family/friends from outside the University.

Pricing

Standard: £115
A Standard ticket provides each guest with full access to all areas of the event, with entry beginning at 9pm.
Queue Jump: £130
A Queue Jump ticket provides each guest with priority queuing, with full access to all areas of the event from 9pm onward.
Dining: £155
A Dining ticket provides each guest with an exclusive private drinks reception beginning at 6:30pm followed by a sumptuous three-course dining experience, with full access to all areas of the event from 9pm onward.

Ents & Food

Experience a night filled with fantastic food and drink at Homerton May Ball. All catering has been specifically selected to complement the theme, allowing you to indulge in style while enjoying the music and entertainment on offer. We will be offering up an inclusive variety of food catering to all dietary requirements, and plenty of both alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks to suit every taste.

Those who choose the dining upgrade will be treated to a champagne reception, followed by a lavish three course meal and early entrance into the May Ball itself!

Amidst the feasting, guests are invited to experience the magic of the fair. With a wide array of entertainment we can assure that you will be spoilt for choice. However you wish to spend your evening, there will be myriad choices of entertainment, carefully tailored to the theme. Whether it be dancing 'til dawn or trying your hand at traditional funfair games, there won’t be any danger of running out of entertainment to enjoy!

Charity

The Homerton May Ball Committee 2017 has chosen to support the following charities:

Wintercomfort

Wintercomfort is a local, Cambridge-based charity that has been supporting homeless men and women for over 25 years. The charity offers a safe haven and provides access to basic amenities such as meals, showers and laundry facilities. They also provide a number of opportunities for learning and training, as well as access to health services and legal advice.

Schistosomiasis Control Initiative

SCI aims to help those who suffer from, and are at risk of, neglected tropical diseases in Sub-Saharan Africa. The charity assists Ministries of Health across the region to control and eliminate schistosomiasis and other diseases in children and adults, utilising the World Health Organization’s Drug Donation Programme. SCI has been included in the GiveWell list of top charities since December 2014.
